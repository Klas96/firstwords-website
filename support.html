Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a flash card?

Open the app, go to "Manage Cards," and add a new card. You can give it a word, attach a photo (from your camera or gallery), and add a sound.

How many cards can I make for free?

You can create up to 20 cards for free. A one-time premium upgrade unlocks unlimited cards — there are no subscriptions.

I bought premium but it's not showing. What do I do?

Open the app and use the "Restore" option in settings to restore your purchase. If it still doesn't appear, email us and we'll help.

Where is my data stored?

All your cards, photos, and sounds are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual cards within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data from your device.
