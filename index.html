First Words

Interactive Flash Cards for Kids

About the App

First Words helps babies and toddlers discover language, one card at a time. Create your own flash cards with photos, words, and sounds — built around the people, pets, and things your child already loves.

Features

  • Make your own cards — add photos, text, and audio to build a personal vocabulary.
  • See it, hear it, learn it — play sounds with the word, the image, or both.
  • Simple & distraction-free — no ads, big friendly cards, easy for tiny hands.
  • Cozy themes — Default, Wood, and Dark Wood.
  • Private by design — all your data stays on your device.
